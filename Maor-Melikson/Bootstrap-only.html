Maor Melikson

The men who returned the proud to an entire city

Maor as the team leader of "Hapoel Beer-Sheva" football team in the championship season.

Here is a timeline of Maor melikson's life:

  • 1984: born in Yavne, Israel.
  • 2001: Started to play in the youth department of Maccabi Yavne.
  • 2002: Moved to adults team of Beitar-Jerusalem after Maccabi yavne were relegated to Liga Bet.
  • 2006: Moved to Maccabi Heifa after not reaching a financial settlement with Beitar Jerusalem.
  • 2008: During the season, Maccabi Haifa sold Melikson to Hapoel Kfar Saba.
  • 2008: After just six months which included a relegation to Liga Leumit, he signed in June 2008 a five-year contract with Hapoel Be'er Sheva .
  • 2011: Joined Polish Ekstraklasa side Wisła Kraków.
  • 2013: Joined Ligue 1 club Valenciennes.
  • 2014: It was announced that Melikson will be back in Beer Sheva, After a big campaign in social medias to bring him back.
  • 2016: Won the Israeli championship with Hapoel-Beer-Sheva after 40 years the team didnt won the championship.
  • 2017: Did it again and won the Israeli championship with Hapoel-Beer-Sheva.
